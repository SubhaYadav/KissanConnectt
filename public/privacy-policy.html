Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 15, 2024

KissanConnect ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our agricultural platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us:

  • Personal information (name, phone number, location)
  • Account credentials (phone number and password)
  • Crop listings and marketplace data
  • Usage data and preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process your transactions and listings
  • Send you important updates and notifications
  • Protect against fraudulent or unauthorized activity
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage

All user data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage technology. We do not transmit your personal information to external servers except for essential services like weather data and map functionality.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your information may be visible to other users only when:

  • You list a crop for sale (your name, phone, and location)
  • Other users contact you regarding your listings

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Object to processing of your data

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services for:

  • Weather data and forecasts
  • Map and location services
  • Insurance and government program information

These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
